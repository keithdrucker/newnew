The Risk Register already exists with a board view (Identified → Under Analysis → Under Treatment → Mitigation → Accepted → Transferred → Avoided → Closed) and a modal detail view with tabs.
Your task is to simplify and structure the Risk Analysis and Treatment workflow, inspired by a detailed spreadsheet model, while making it intuitive and low‑friction for daily use.

Constraints:

✅ Keep the existing board layout
✅ Keep the existing risk lifecycle states
✅ Keep the existing modal + tab structure
❌ Do NOT introduce Risk Register as a Work Type
❌ Do NOT convert risks into tasks or tickets

You are refining, not redesigning.

Overview
Analysis
Treatment
Linked Work
History

Yes / No

Quantitative Asset Value (Optional numeric)

Low / Medium / High / Very High

Fields:

Threats

Vulnerabilities

Multi‑select or short bullet entries
